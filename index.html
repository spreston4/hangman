Hangman

Hangman is a word guessing game where you will attempt to build a missing word by guessing one letter at a time. The game ends when you guess the word, or you run out of guesses. You start with 10 guesses. An incorrect guess will decrease your remaining guesses by 1 & add a body part to the hanging person. If you correctly identify all letters of the missing word, you win! If you run out of guesses, you lose. Guess the word to save a life! Press the ‘Play!’ button to begin!
